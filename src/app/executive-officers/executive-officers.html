Executive Officers & Board of Directors

Our Executive team and Board of Directors provide direction and leadership for the operations of The Warrior Leader Foundation. to ensure we deliver excellent programs for America's military and first responders.

We are blessed to have an amazing group of individuals providing direction and leadership to our foundation. In addition to serving on our Board of Directors, members are also expected to be a leader by providing their specific expertise and advice with The Warrior Leader Foundation's daily operations and formation of Local Chapters.

Brandon Amerine is the Founder, President, and CEO of The Warrior Leader Foundation. He is currently serving Active Duty in the United States Army as a Rifle Platoon Leader with the 82nd Airborne Division. He was born and raised in Muscatine, Iowa where he was very involved in sports growing up and where he also decided to join the Army in 2009. Brandon is responsible for the strategic direction of TWLF, which includes administration of the board, program development, fundraising management, and ensuring that the organization and its partnerships are aligned with TWLF's mission. Brandon graduated with a Bachelors Degree in Business Management and commissioned into Active Duty from Iowa State University in Ames, IA. He currently resides in Fayetteville, NC with his wife.
