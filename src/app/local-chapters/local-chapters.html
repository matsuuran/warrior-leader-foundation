Chapter Programs

Our chapter Program is the most essential part of our foundation. They provide local communities the opportunity to build meaningful relationships and support systems for and with their local military and first responders through physical, community and social activities. Each chapter has 6 functional leaders that run and coordinate events for the TWLF chapter and ensure organizational involvement within their communities. Each chapter is required to have following leaders to start:

  • Chapter Leader - Responsible for the TWLF Chapter and its success. Tracks events, membership, and support from local community.
  • Warrior Leader - Ensures outreach and involvement from local military and first responders and their families.
  • Athletic Leader - Plans and coordinates events like: Crossfit workouts, sports, obbstacle races, and anything physical.
  • Outdoors Leader - Responsible for organizing events like hunting, fishing, camping, hiking, paintball, and motorcycle rides as a chapter.
  • Community Leader - Responsible for outreach to local organizations for volunteer opportunities and other community involvement.
  • Social Leader - Plans events like BBQs, dinner events, get-togethers with local police, fire unions, and other veteran's organizations.

Local Chapters are a great way for current military, veterans, and first responders to connect through various activities such as:

Physical Events

  • Running Events
  • Biking
  • Sports
  • Crossfit Workouts
  • Kit Runs
  • Ruck Marches
  • Yoga/Zumba

Community Involvement

  • Volunteering
  • College Veteran Affairs Programs
  • High School JROTC
  • High School Sports
  • Involvement with Boy/Girl Scouts
  • Youth Sports Programs

Social Events

  • BBQs
  • Lunches & Coffee Meetups for Spouses
  • VFW & American Legion Events
  • Concealed Carry Classes with Local Police
  • Self-Defense Classes

Outdoor Activites

  • Hunting
  • Fishing
  • Hiking
  • Rock Climbing
  • Motorcycle Riding
  • Paintball/Milsim/Airsoft Outings

The above are just ideas and opportunities that we have come up with to aid and assist the start-up and ongoing function of a TWLF chapter. if you are involved with a local chapter, please do not limit yourself to what we have listed above. Chapters are supposed to be personalized and should meet the wants and needs of their respective communities while staying within the guidelines and culture of the TWLF National Chapter.
