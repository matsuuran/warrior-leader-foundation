Mission

Background

The Warrior Leader Foundation, Inc. was founded by a United States Army Infantry Officer serving in the prestigious 82nd Airborne Division in 2015. With the help and assistance from an Associate of the American Red Cross, they developed an idea for a Nonprofit organization dedicated towards uniting America's military and first responders communities through their common WARRIOR SPIRIT.

Although military and first responders have different roles within American society, they share a common characteristic in that both willingly raised their right hands in service to something bigger than themselves.

The safety and strength of our country does not come from just one of these organizations working alone. It is a result of BOTH, military and first responders, working together towards a common goal, that is: Keeping America and it's citizens safe and free from harm's way.

Our Vision

It is our vision to create a FUN, STRONG and SUPPORTIVE community of like-minded individuals dedicated towards HONORING and ASSISTING America's fallen and injured military and first responders through physical, community and social activities.

Our Culture

The Warrior Leader Foundation, Inc. is striving to build a culture and environment of dedication to duty and dedication to service. We want to create a FUN, INVITING, and SUPPORTIVE environment that welcomes all military and first responders who are either serving or are veterans of their respective organizations to build lasting relationships through common interests.

The military and first responder communities are made up of Warriors by nature. They each share a common bond through dedication to organizations that require teamwork, camaraderie and dedication to a common goal. Through these common characteristics, we will foster a culture that is comprised of WARRIORS in their professions and LEADERS in their communities.

How We Do This

We utilize the relationships we have built within or organization and local communities to raise awareness of our cause through physical, community and social activities such as:

  • Running Events
  • Sports
  • Crossfit Workouts
  • Ruck Marches
  • Shooting Events/Competitions
  • Volunteer Work
  • Hosting Food/Clothing/Toy Drives
  • BBQs and Events for Children

Through these events, we will work to raise funds and awareness to support military, first responders and their families who have fallen or been injured in the line of duty.
